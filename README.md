# Swiggy Sales Analytics 

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze Swiggy food delivery sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends across India.

---

## 1. Short Description / Purpose
This Excel dashboard helps analyze Swiggy’s sales data by tracking revenue, orders, ratings, and regional performance.  
It enables stakeholders to identify sales trends, top-performing locations, and customer preferences for better business decisions.

---

## 2. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and techniques:

- **Microsoft Excel** – Main dashboard creation and visualization platform  
- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Data aggregation and analysis  
- **Excel Formulas** – KPI calculations and data logic  
- **Slicers & Filters** – Interactive filtering by month, state, and restaurant  
- **Excel Map Chart** – State-wise sales visualization  
- **File Format** – `.xlsx` for dashboard, `.png` for previews  

---

## 3. Data Source
- Simulated Swiggy food delivery sales dataset  
- Data includes:
  - Order details
  - Revenue
  - Customer ratings
  - Food type (Veg / Non-Veg)
  - City and State information
  - Time-based data (Daily, Monthly, Quarterly)

*(Dataset used for analytical and learning purposes.)*

---

## 4. Features / Highlights

### Business Problem
Swiggy needs a centralized view to monitor sales performance, customer satisfaction, and regional demand patterns.

### Goal of the Dashboard
- Track key sales and order KPIs
- Understand customer rating trends
- Identify top cities and high-performing states
- Analyze sales behavior across time periods

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Orders, Average Rating, AOV  
- Monthly, Daily, and Weekly Sales Trends  
- Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)  
- State-wise Sales Map  
- Quarterly Performance Summary  
- Top 5 Cities by Sales  

### Business Impact & Insights
- Non-Veg food contributes the highest revenue share  
- Weekend sales outperform weekdays  
- Metro cities dominate overall revenue contribution  
- Customer ratings remain stable across quarters
